Course Details
A. Aircraft Systems:

Gain an in-depth understanding of the complex systems that comprise the A320 aircraft. This includes detailed knowledge of its electrical, hydraulic, and pneumatic systems.

B. Flying Techniques:

Master the fundamental flying techniques unique to the A320, covering essential skills such as takeoff, landing, and maneuvering in diverse flight conditions.

C. Flow Patterns:

Learn to execute systematic flow patterns in cockpit operations, ensuring efficiency and precision in flight procedures.

D. Sequences:

Understand the sequential order of actions and tasks required for various flight phases, from pre-flight checks to post-flight shutdown procedures.

E. Procedures:

Become familiar with the standard operating procedures (SOPs) and protocols specific to the A320, ensuring the safe and efficient operation of the aircraft.

F. MCDU Preparation:

Develop proficiency in using the Multi-Function Control and Display Unit (MCDU), essential for managing flight plans, performance calculations, and system configurations.

G. Comprehensive Notes:

Access detailed notes from the Flight Crew Operating Manual (FCOM), Flight Crew Training Manual (FCTM), and Quick Reference Handbook (QRH), providing valuable references throughout your training journey.
